## Journal Manuscript

### Springback in V-Bending: Role of Loading, Force-Holding Time, and Heat Treatment

**Thouhidul Islam Rajon and collaborators**

**Status:** Manuscript submitted to *The International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology*

This study investigated the effects of applied load, force-holding time, and heat treatment on springback during V-bending.

The experimental work included:

- Aluminum
- Mild steel
- Stainless steel
- Annealing
- Normalizing
- Quenching
- Hardness measurements
- Springback-angle measurements
- Comparison of loading conditions

The findings showed that higher applied loads and longer force-holding times generally reduced springback. Annealing also reduced springback by lowering material hardness.

## Undergraduate Thesis

### Springback in V-Bending: Role of Loading, Force-Holding Time, and Heat Treatment

**Shahjalal University of Science and Technology**

The thesis examined how processing conditions influence springback in sheet-metal bending.

The work involved:

- Designing a 90-degree V-die and punch
- Conducting bending experiments
- Measuring springback angles
- Studying applied force and holding time
- Comparing heat-treatment conditions
- Evaluating changes in material hardness
- Analyzing experimental results
