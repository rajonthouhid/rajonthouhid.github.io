## 3D-Printed Porous Structures for Compost Aeration

**Master’s Thesis Research**  
**Grand Valley State University**

This research investigates the design and experimental evaluation of 3D-printed aeration structures for laboratory-scale composting systems.

### Key Activities

- Designed porous aeration structures
- Created geometries using nTopology and CAD tools
- Fabricated PLA components using FDM 3D printing
- Measured airflow using the water-displacement method
- Conducted aerated and non-aerated compost experiments
- Monitored temperature, carbon dioxide, relative humidity, and mass
- Prepared experimental documentation and CFD analysis plans

### Tools and Technologies

`nTopology` · `CAD` · `FDM 3D Printing` · `LabQuest 3` · `Experimental Testing` · `CFD`

## Springback in V-Bending

**Undergraduate Thesis**  
**Shahjalal University of Science and Technology**

This research investigated the effects of loading force, force-holding time, and heat treatment on springback during V-bending.

### Materials Studied

- Aluminum
- Mild steel
- Stainless steel

### Heat-Treatment Conditions

- Annealing
- Normalizing
- Quenching

### Key Activities

- Designed a 90-degree V-die and punch
- Conducted bending experiments
- Measured springback angles
- Evaluated the effects of applied force
- Studied the influence of holding time
- Compared heat-treatment conditions
- Measured hardness changes
- Analyzed experimental results

### Main Findings

Higher loading forces and longer holding times generally reduced springback. Annealing reduced material hardness and also decreased springback.

### Tools and Methods

`Metal Forming` · `Experimental Mechanics` · `Heat Treatment` · `Hardness Testing` · `Data Analysis`
